Introduction

Jennifer Jane Jewelry

Jennifer Jane is a small, personable jewelry business started by two sisters in the heart of Laguna Beach, California. Our passion is to create timeless, quality pieces that you'll live in and layer every day! What started as an assembly team of two on our apartment floor is now our prized store location dedicated to serving the needs of our Laguna Beach community. Stop by! We'll have you ready for any occasion.

Expression and Styles for Everyone

Jennifer Jane serves a consumer base primarily composed of women with an interest in style and a value for quality pieces. This can be mother, grandmothers, daughters, grandaughters, sisters, friends, etc.

We make pieces that stand the test of time: These can be for women of all walks of life (working, business savy, family caretakers, students, bakers, gardeners,absolutely anyone who can express themselves through style. Their primary goal in coming to us is to find jewelry that showcases who they are. They would visit this site in search of everyday pieces, gifts, accessories for a special event, etc.
