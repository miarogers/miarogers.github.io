Wild House Paper Co. Company Feature

Every WH piece is an original work of art that is unique to our line. Intentionality is at the heart of our brand as it makes each piece a valuable addition to your curated lifestyle. When you buy a WH good, you are not only going to receive a quality item but you are supporting the slow process of art that really is so unique in today’s industry.

Created in California using the highest quality sustainable materials, our paper products are printed on luxe recycled paper stocks. From our paper stock to our packaging materials, we are dedicated to choosing sustainable choices whenever possible (and there is still a lot of work to be done). All of the paper in our line is recycled and that's important to us. Sustainability and intentional decisions are at the forefront of our design process from concept to final packaging. From our paper mills located in Michigan, Boston + New York to our printing partners spread across California, all of our products are 100% made in the USA.

Through our partner, One Tree Planted, a nonprofit organization with a focus on global reforestation, we have committed to planting one tree for every order placed. Our hope is that these efforts not only give our community a simple way to give back, but also put resources back into our Earth that our goods are made of. We have chosen to offer the option to plant a tree in three locations that are currently in need : California, the Amazon Rainforest + New South Wales, Australia.

Wilde House Paper comes to life through the creative duo of Megan Heddinger + Connor Drechsler. Based out of their studio in San Luis Obispo, California, they bring different sides to the business that allow for a perfect partnership.

Megan Heddinger is a multifaceted creative who is the visionary behind all things design.Coming from a background in fashion and graphic design, Megan mixes the elements of minimal design with a hand touch that result in elevated products and designs. Inspired by color, texture, art, style and California surroundings, Wilde House comes to life to complement an artistic lifestyle. Every piece in the line is concepted, designed and created by Megan and her small creative team.Connor Drechsler is an intuitive “generalist” who is all realms of business for WH. Leaving his corporate career path, Connor took what started as a side project into the thriving business you see WH as today. A perfect balance of left and right brain, Connor leads the team forward in all things wholesale, finance and production. Inspired by structure, minimalism and the California coast, Connor is the backbone to WH that allows the creative team to be at their fullest potential.
